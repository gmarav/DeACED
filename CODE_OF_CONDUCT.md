# Code of Conduct

DeACED aims to be a welcoming, professional project for everyone who takes part
in it.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and considerate in all interactions.
- Give and accept constructive feedback in good faith.
- Focus on what is best for the project and its users.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks.
- Publishing others' private information without their permission.
- Any conduct that would be inappropriate in a professional setting.

## Reporting and enforcement

Report unacceptable behavior privately to the project maintainer,
[@gmarav](https://github.com/gmarav); reports are handled discreetly.
Maintainers may edit or remove contributions that violate this code, and may
warn, temporarily restrict, or permanently ban anyone for repeated or serious
violations.
